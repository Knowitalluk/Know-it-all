Rules & How to Play

Know It All? is a live quiz for up to 20 players per room.

• 20 seconds per question. Players tap one answer (locked).
• Correct answers earn up to 100 points based on speed.

Streak perk (every 5 correct): choose 2× Score or Sabotage.
Sabotage blocks a player from answering the next question.
Game pauses until all perk players choose.

CHEAT (1): instantly selects the correct answer.
HELP (3): removes 2 wrong answers immediately (50/50).

Voice chat: press Voice Chat (Jitsi audio room).
